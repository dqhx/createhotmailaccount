Create a Hotmail Account

There are a lot of reasons why you should take into consideration using the email service provided by Hotmail.com In order to take advantage of using the service, you have to create a Hotmail.com account. Currently known as Outlook, Microsoft renamed its email service in 2013 it boasts a reliable security system, an effective system of filtering spam, and a simple, nice interface that allows to easily manage one's email account. Furthermore, there is a possibility to connect one's Hotmail.com account with Outlook, enabling to look through one's letters offline.

Create Hotmail Account Step-by-Step


  1. Thanks to Microsoft, which included Hotmail to the MSN set of programs, you are able to enter the website of Hotmail right from there (among other products, Hotmail is named as Outlook.com). It is much easier, however, to open one of the following few websites in your browser: http://hotmail.com or http://outlook.com. This way, you appear on the Hotmail's main page, shown in the screenshot.


    2. Continue to the Hotmail Login Page


  2. Once you see the window with the website's main page opened in front of you, click on "Create one!" near the "No account?" question, placed right below the blue "Sign in" button.

  3. After having done so, you can see a registration form opened in your browsers. You have to type your information in. At first, enter your real name, since it would stand in good stead if you will forget your password or your data will be stolen (if you are neglectful, for instance).

    Then, choose the name of your email account and type it in. Following it, make up a password secure enough, which is difficult to hack (it is recommended to not use the birthdate or any memorable dates). Move on and choose your region, specify your date of birth as well as gender, and make sure that your data will be protected by providing your phone number for the sake of security (it would actually come in handy if this is your main email account).

    Instead of providing the phone number, however, you can specify another existing email address of yours. With the help of it, you will be able to restore the forgotten or stolen Hotmail password.

  4. The registration process to create hotmail account is almost finished. Now, you have to prove that you are not a robot. In order to do that, enter the symbols from the picture (in the bottom part of the registration form) in the field beneath it. If you have faced a problem, substitute the picture with another one (click on "New") or listen to it (click on "Audio"). If even those actions did not help, activate your account with the help of the SMS message sent to your phone.

  5. Ultimately, click on the "Sign up" button and the process is completed! You will see the logo of Outlook and the icon of downloading (the way as shown in the screenshot), and in several minutes you will be able to use your new email account. As this step-by-step guide showed, it is really not that difficult to create a Hotmail account.
